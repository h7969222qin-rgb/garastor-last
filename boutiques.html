Where European Design

Our factory in Serbia is where design becomes floor. Drawing from the deep well of Italian culture and craftsmanship traditions, every GARASTOR floor is born in a state-of-the-art European manufacturing facility — a place where heritage, precision, and the finest European oak converge.

European Design

The Culture That Shapes Every Collection

Italy gave the world more than pasta and opera. It gave the world a way of thinking about making — a philosophy where beauty and utility are inseparable, where proportion is a form of truth, and where the hand and the eye work in concert.

GARASTOR draws from this heritage. Our palettes, our textures, our very understanding of what a floor should be — these are shaped by the culture of Italian design. Every collection begins as a meditation on form, proportion, and the relationship between the material and the space it inhabits.

Design & Direction

Serbia

The Factory

Amid the oak forests of the Balkans stands GARASTOR's manufacturing headquarters. This is where design becomes floor. Where every plank is milled, graded, finished, and inspected to exacting standards.

Balkan oak is among the finest in Europe — dense, stable, with a grain that responds beautifully to our finishing techniques. Our Serbian base gives us direct access to this extraordinary material, while providing efficient distribution across the continent and beyond.
